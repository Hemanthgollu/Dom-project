KHAIDI

Movie is about in order to kill a group of five cops who seize crores worth of drugs a drug-dealing gang targets the police intelligence office with the help of an informer. In the process, they supply drugs based liquor to a private police party. To rescue them an officer named Vijay takes the help of a just-released prisoner Delhi.
