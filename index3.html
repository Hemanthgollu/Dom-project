LEO

Parthiban lives in Himachal Pradesh with his wife Sathya, and their two children, where they peacefully operate a coffee shop. However, his life takes a dramatic turn when he becomes a local hero due to a courageous act, attracting the attention of Antony Das and Harold Das. They suspect him of being their estranged relative, Leo Das.
